Coin Control — The Teen Budget App That Actually Makes Sense

Coin Control is a free, beautifully designed budget tracker built for teenagers who want to take control of their money. Whether you're managing your weekly allowance, saving up for something big, or splitting bills with friends, Coin Control makes it simple, visual, and even a little fun.
Track every dollar coming in and going out, set a weekly spending limit, and get instant alerts before you blow your budget. Watch your savings goals grow with live progress bars — and celebrate with a confetti burst when you finally hit one. Need to split a restaurant bill? The built-in calculator handles tips, divides it evenly, and tracks who's paid.
With colour-coded spending charts, a clean dashboard, and a design that works on both mobile and desktop, Coin Control turns money management from a chore into something you'll actually want to open every day.
Key Features:

Income and expense tracking with categories
Weekly budget limit with real-time alerts at 80% and over-budget
Savings goals with progress bars and confetti celebrations 🎉
Split bill calculator with tip support and who's-paid tracking
Spending charts by category and income vs expenses
Push notifications to keep you on track
Works offline — all data saved locally on your device
Dark and light mode
Free, no account needed, no ads
